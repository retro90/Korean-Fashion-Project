̊About Korean Fashion


In recent years, fashion in South Korea has greatly evolved, due to inspiration from the West. Factors that have influenced the changes in South Korean fashion are culture, wealth, and (social) media. The country's developing economy has also had a profound effect on fashion. Currently, South Korea maintains a unique fashion style that has become an acknowledged influence in worldwide trends. South Korea's style has become more expressive, reflecting a sens of individuality, which is absent from the style of its northern counterpart North Korea.

̊Korean Designers

Moon Young Hee

is a designer who expresses Korean ideas modernistically. The world can share her designs. By the end of the 1960s, she had worked as a designer in Wha Shin Renaun, and she founded the 'Moon Boutique' ready-made clothing brand in 1974. In 1992, she founded the 'Mood Young Hee' designer brand.

Andre Kim

was a South Korean fashion designer based in Seoul, South Korea. He was k nown predominantly for his evening and wedding gown collections. He is survived by his adopted son, Kim Jung-do.

Lie Sang-Bong

Lie Sang-Bong is a major fashion designer who shows Korean fashions in pret-a-porter, which is the core of the global fashion industry.

Kim Jae-Hyun

The founder of Jardin de Chouette, a brand that has successfully garnered a large fan base thanks to its nostalgic applique paterns and feminine cuts. Jae-hyun later started a sister label called Lucky Chouette the same year Jardin de Chouette was founded (in 2005). The sister label lured more younger customers with its afforable price.

Kiok Kang

Founder of Kiok in 2004, the brand is famously known for its use of demin in its collection. The brand truly apppeals to the younger generations who prefer a good dose of old-school cool with contemporary charm in their wardrobe.

YK and Do

Human Potential or HUPOT was founded by YK and DO in 2008. With head-to-toe- black and monochrome looks as the brand's signature style, Human Potential's celebrity fans include US rappers A$AP Rocky and Big Sean.
